About ELAN Dome

ELAN Dome Consultants is an Africa based, bred and led educational, social & corporate outsourcing consultancy firm that strives to reshape the path of global education and international business engagements for students, individuals and institutions interested in integrating international experience and community service by offering high-quality international volunteer and service-learning engagements.

Additionally, ELAN Dome Consultants offers strategic advisory services, organizational programming, project management, external quality assurance checks, curriculum research and development and international students collaboration projects. Our aim is to increase quality education accessibility and enhance the success rate for Africa based interventions by promoting cultural exchange and holistic learning.

Our Mission 

To match prospective individuals and institutions with communities and organizations through global networking and partnerships.

Our Vision 

Experiential learning beyond borders

Client Focus Goal 

Optimized logistical support, cultural experiences and fulfillment to the stakeholders.

Primary Goal 

To facilitate a seamless and targeted positioning of individuals, families, groups and institutions in local but impactful initiatives in Africa.
