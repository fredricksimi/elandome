{% trans "Volunteer Services" %}

{% blocktrans %} The OBC Volunteer program is ideal for students and practitioners and aims to place enthusiastic volunteers in the right spaces to make a positive impact on the lives of underserved people within local African Communities. The OBC volunteers get to interact with the local communities and immerse themselves in rich African culture. Not only do they experience the fulfillment of making positive changes to these underprivileged communities, they also develop skills through a multicultural lens and a global spectrum. {% endblocktrans %}

The OBC Volunteer program is open to individuals across the board from different backgrounds such as:

  1. General Health Care
  2. Urban Planning and Development
  3. Economic Empowerment
  4. Water and Sanitation
  5. Eco Farming
  6. Mental Health
  7. Youth
  8. Education
  9. Child Care
  10. Elderly Care

{% blocktrans %} We give our volunteers a detailed overview of ELAN Dome's volunteer missions and expectations to enable them to be confident and knowledgeable about the causes they are undertaking and to cultivate the motivation and enthusiasm that encouraged them to volunteer in the first place. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Due to the rich farming community in Kenya, eco farming volunteering is highly encouraged for individuals who are concerned about sustainability, climate change and community farming. They get to connect with the local farming communities and share their skills to enhance the farming standards and contribute to food security. {% trans "Investment supervision, coordination and implementation" %}

{% blocktrans %}We support local citizens in the diaspora and international development organizations with all their investment needs. Using our knowledge about the local market we prioritize efficient and seamless operations that protect them from negative eventualities. Through our investment supervision services, we offer our local and international investor's representative service and supervision of their investment projects thereby enhancing accountability.{% endblocktrans %}

These services range from:

  1. Supporting managing the entire investment process.
  2. Regularly inspecting investments sites in order to control the progress.
  3. Verifying work carried out contractors.
  4. Generating post-completion documentation.
  5. Taking part in coordination meetings.
  6. Preparing and taking part in the final handover of the investment to the investor.

{% trans "Project Management" %}

{% blocktrans %} We handle all aspects pertaining to the planning and execution of projects. We monitor projects through the stages of project initiation, planning, execution, and closure to make sure that the objectives of the project are fulfilled. We manage projects effectively so that they are completed on time, to the set specification and within the stipulated budgets. With our strong grasp of various modes of project delivery we can identify and recommend the most appropriate project delivery approach for projects. {% endblocktrans %}

Some of our project management services include:

  1. Define program goals and project scope
  2. Identify project delivery methods
  3. Outline project schedules
  4. Cost management
  5. Develop and Evaluate proposals
  6. Manage Consulting Services
  7. Develop Contract Documentation
  8. Draft and generate project reports
