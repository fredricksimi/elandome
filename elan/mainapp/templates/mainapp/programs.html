Volunteer Services

The OBC volunteer program was created in honor of the Late Engineer Okore Booker Charles. This program has been carefully curated to reflect and inculcate the values that he lived by throughout his life in community service and social impact.

The OBC volunteer program will help you render assistance to underprivileged and underserved communities where you will experience traditional cultures and even get a chance to interact with rich wildlife all while supporting a good cause and creating a positive impact in the society.
